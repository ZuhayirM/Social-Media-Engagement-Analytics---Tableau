# Social Media Engagement Analytics (Tableau)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes social media engagement patterns using Tableau dashboards and visual storytelling techniques. The goal is to identify the key factors that drive audience engagement across different platforms, emotions, campaign phases, and geographic regions.

## Dataset
The dataset used in this project is the **Social Media Engagement Dataset** sourced from Kaggle.

The dataset contains simulated social media post metrics including:

- Likes
- Comments
- Shares
- Impressions
- Sentiment score
- Emotional tone
- Platform
- Campaign phase
- Brand
- Location

Additional calculated fields were created to enhance analysis, including:

- Engagement Rate
- Posting Hour
- Time of Day
- Posting Weekday
- Country and City extraction
- Dominant Emotion

## Research Questions
This project answers several key marketing analytics questions:

1. How does engagement vary across content categories and weekdays?
2. How does engagement change over time by sentiment?
3. What time of day generates the most engagement?
4. Which emotions drive higher engagement?
5. How does engagement vary across campaign phases?
6. How does sentiment vary across brands and platforms?
7. How do emotions influence campaign performance?
8. Which countries show the highest engagement rates?

## Key Insights

### Timing and Content Strategy
- Product-related posts perform best mid-week.
- Delivery-related content performs best on weekends.
- Evening posts (6–9 PM) drive the highest engagement on Facebook and Instagram.

### Emotional Influence
- Positive emotions such as **Happy** and **Excited** generate the highest engagement.
- Negative emotions such as **Angry** and **Sad** consistently underperform.

### Platform Behavior
- Twitter and Reddit show wider sentiment variation and more extreme reactions.
- Facebook and Instagram maintain more neutral sentiment distributions.

### Campaign Performance
- Engagement peaks during **Launch** and **Post-Launch** campaign phases.

### Geographic Trends
- South Korea, Canada, and the UK show higher engagement rates compared to other countries.
